Cereal

Description

One of the best ways to start your day, aside from coffee of course, cereal does a great job at filling you up, getting you going, all while being of the easiest things to make!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cereal - about 1 cup or a bowlful
  • Your favorite milk - just enough to cover the cereal

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl
  2. Pour cereal into the bowl
  3. Pour milk into bowl
  4. Enjoy!

Also feel free to try out other cereals and other types of milk!
