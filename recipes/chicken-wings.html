Chicken Wings

Description

Wonderful tasty treats that the whole family can enjoy. Goes great at any time and can be as mild or as spicy as you like them!

Ingredients

  • For Chicken Wings
    • Chicken Wings - 3 pounds
    • Salt - pinch of
    • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • For Sauce
    • Unsalted Butter - 1/2 cup (8 tablespoons)
    • Franks Red Hot Sauce - 3/4 cup (can use other brands)
    • Worcestershire sauce - 2 teaspoons
    • Salt - 1 teaspoon
    • Sugar - 1 teaspoon
    • Garlic Powder - 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. For Sauce
    1. Mix all sauce ingredients together on low heat. Use whisk once the butter melts. Set aside once done.
  2. For Chicken
    1. Cut the wings if needed.
    2. Heat the grill to about 250 F or 300 F. This will allow the fat to slowly drain out.
    3. Grill the wings with Vegetable oil and salt. cover and cook for about 30 minutes
    4. Baste the wings with the sauce from before. Every 15 minutes turn the wings and baste again. Ensure there is some sauce left over at the end.
    5. Once done throw chicken wings and last bit of the sauce into a bowl and toss. Serve and enjoy.
